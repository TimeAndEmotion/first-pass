Overview

Estmation is a pain. A necessity but still a pain. Like foreplay is to sex.

In software teams estimation is done by measuring the perceived complexity of a task.

Complexity can be split into design and effort to deliver. The design may be forwards or backwards when reverse engineering is needed.

This simple realisation allows the task to be profiled as design and delivery phases. They are preceeded by a scan phase and succeeded by a value phase. It looks like this.

At the slight risk of sounding hereticall to the accepted wisdom of team players I ask you to consider personal value.
Subsequent versions will incorporate four different values: Customer, Strategic, Operational and Team. For now put yourself first!

The way I start estimating is by doing a gut feel pass to associate profiles with the task. It is very quick. A day or two later I return and pay attention to tasks that need more consideration.

Aim - Get feedback on ease of use for first pass estimate
